:Title:
FiveShpheres

:ShortDescription:
An demonstration of different primitive types.

:FullDescription:	
This demo displays five shpheres displaying five different primitive types:
	-	GL_POINTS
	-	GL_TRIANGLES
	-	GL_LINE_STRIP
	-	GL_TRIANGLE_FAN
	-	GL_TRIANGLE_STRIP
